Eggplant ($EGGPLANT) is a token on Binance Smart Chain possessing a number of exciting features. Eggplant features a unique deflationary design that exponentially decreases the total supply with every transaction made.

Eggplant is a decentralized project, meaning it is completlely community led and created by volunteers. There are no founder/developer wallets, however a small portion of tokens (7.5%) are reserved for marketing and development purposes. Over 50% of tokens are burned forever, and the remaining circulating supply is available on the open market. We have added a 1% max transaction amount to avoid large transactions and price manipulation.
